Obtaining Coordinates and Link

To get longitude and latitude coordinates, right-click on the desired location in Google Maps, select "What's here?", and copy the displayed coordinates. For a shareable link, click the "Share" button in Google Maps, ensuring accurate location information and easy access to the site.
